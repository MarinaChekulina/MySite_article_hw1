Industry support for Wi-Fi Aware is widespread Smartphones, tablets, and a variety of other consumer electronics products stand to benefit from the enhanced capabilities of Wi-Fi Aware, and silicon vendors have begun to certify the core technology that will power next-generation mobile devices.

The first Wi-Fi CERTIFIED Wi-Fi Aware products which form the test suite for the certification program are:

  • Broadcom BCM4358
  • Intel® Dual Band Wireless-AC 7260
  • Marvell Avastar 88W8897 802.11ac low power Wi-Fi combo chip
  • Realtek RTL8812AE 2x2 a/b/g/n/ac MiniCard
